## Findings And Corrections

### Canonical architecture omitted invocation output

`android-fsm-architecture.md` still described
`AfsmTransition(newState, commands, effects)` and said the ViewModel directly
updates StateFlow and executes commands. That was the pre-runtime conceptual
shape and contradicted the current host boundary.

Correction: include `commandInvocations` and `decision`, make `AfsmHost` the
runtime publisher/executor, and describe the ViewModel as lifecycle/handler
integration.

### Official Android mapping used only ordinary commands

`android-official-guidance.md` still mapped all async work to `Command` and did
not distinguish local phase-owned cooperative work.

Correction: retain ViewModel/viewModelScope ownership while documenting
sequential commands versus phase-owned invocation and the remote-cancellation
boundary.

### Current and historical separation

`current-state.md`, `open-questions.md`, public docs, and the current runtime
page agree on invocation and privacy contracts. The old reference architecture
review is explicitly marked `historical-review`; early API drafts remain
historical in the index. Their old terms are preserved as design evidence.
